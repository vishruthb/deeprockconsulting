Why DeepRock

DeepRock is different in many aspects of business consulting. DeepRock works with each client to ensure that strategies that are developed and campaigns that are implemented are methodically organized for maxiumum potential.

Data-Based

Every consultational advice and decision we make on our client's behalf is backed up with data and models.

We implement the use of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning models, namely logistical and linear regression models as well as Naive Bayes models to pinpoint trajectories of business's profits, market engagement, etc.

These models are generated by the use of data points of financial assets, social media/website (online) engagement, and profits of past campaigns and pivots in business model.

Goal-Oriented

The strategies that DeepRock develops for clients is based off thorough conversations and communication with them, which then we incorporate into how we advice businesses to move forward.

Communication

We will work with clients to set up in-person meetings at the clients' offices or over the phone Zoom calls. These meetings will be etremely productive and thoroughly documented (we will be taking notes) and we will be asking questions that are meant to learn the most about the company such that we can help our clients succeed in their ventures.

New Ways of Thinking

Our consultants are younger and more experienced with the new demographics of customers in today's societies. They each have significant professional knowledge in the fields of business and its' subsets in finance/economics, marketing, sales, and computer science/IT. DeepRock's consultants are able to bring up new and unnoticed strategies and viewpoints that other consulting companies would not necessarily be able to do.
